# Demo Video Script & Guide

**Target Duration:** < 4 minutes

## Part 1: Introduction & Pitch (0:00 - 0:45)

*   **Visual:** Show the landing screen of the Lumi app.
*   **Audio (Voiceover):**
    *   "Hi, I'm [Your Name], and this is Lumi—a next-generation AI tutor powered by Google's Gemini Live API."
    *   "Traditional AI tutors are text-based and slow. Lumi changes that. It's a real-time, multimodal agent that you can talk to naturally, just like a human teacher."
    *   "It solves the problem of disconnected learning by letting the AI 'see' what you're working on and responding instantly with voice, creating a truly immersive educational experience."

## Part 2: Real-time Voice Demo (0:45 - 1:30)

*   **Action:** Click the **Microphone** button to connect.
*   **Visual:** Show the "Connected" state and the pulsing visualizer.
*   **Interaction:**
    *   **You:** "Hey Lumi, I'm trying to understand how black holes work. Can you explain it simply?"
    *   **Lumi:** (Responds with audio)
    *   **You:** (Interrupt Lumi mid-sentence) "Wait, what's an event horizon?"
    *   **Lumi:** (Stops immediately and pivots to explain the event horizon).
*   **Key Point to Mention:** "Notice the low latency and how I can interrupt it naturally. This is powered by Gemini's native audio streaming."

## Part 3: Multimodal Vision Demo (1:30 - 2:30)

*   **Action:** Click the **Video** icon to enable the camera.
*   **Visual:** Show yourself holding an object (e.g., a plant, a book, or a math problem written on paper).
*   **Interaction:**
    *   **You:** "Lumi, take a look at this. What kind of plant is this, and how often should I water it?"
    *   **Lumi:** (Analyzes the video feed and responds with specific advice based on what it sees).
*   **Key Point to Mention:** "Lumi isn't just hearing me; it's seeing my world in real-time through the video stream, allowing for context-aware assistance."

## Part 4: Conclusion & Tech Stack (2:30 - 3:00)

*   **Visual:** Briefly show the code editor (VS Code) or the Architecture Diagram.
*   **Audio (Voiceover):**
    *   "Under the hood, Lumi is built with React and Vite, using the Google GenAI SDK to connect directly to the Gemini 2.5 Flash model on Google Cloud."
    *   "We handle real-time audio processing in the browser to ensure that crisp, instant feedback loop."
    *   "Lumi represents the future of interactive learning—fast, visual, and human-like."
    *   "Thanks for watching!"
